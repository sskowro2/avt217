Special Menu

Explore the options Pines of Nowhere has to offer with our special menu items. The special items are on rotation and are changed out each week. While the specials list may be limited in the number of items, what we specialize in is the quality of our products.

Seasonal Avocado Toast

Enjoy this seasons avocado toast served on freshly baked brioche bread, goat cheese, pickled shallots, alfalfa sprouts, seasoned avocado, a poached egg, and olive oil.

Special Coffee

Try our specially brewed cold-brew coffee. Having steeped for over twenty hours, our cold-brew is dark, creamy, and full of flavor. By changing the roast the cold-brew is made from each week, the taste is always changing.

Assorted Fruit Toast

Try a healthy brunch with our fruit toast. Our fruit options are seasonal and change depending on what the local farms grow, so do ask us what is included in today's fruit toast. Also includes freshly baked whole wheat bread, orange-zest yogurt, and honey.

Stuffed Croissant

Our Stuffed Croissant is a popular item on our menu for good reason. Inside our stuffed croissant is brie, fig, and bacon to tie in a rich brunch treat. We also offer vegetarian and vegan if that would suit you best.

Traditional Avocado Toast

Cooked on fresh brioche bread, our avocado toast is prepared with the highest quality of ingredients. paired with goat cheese and olive oil, one simply cannot beat a traditional avocado toast.
